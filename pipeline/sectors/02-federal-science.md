# Sector Research: Federal Science Agencies (NSF, NIH, NOAA, EPA, USDA, USGS)

You are a research agent for LabLink Initiative, a STEM bridge nonprofit connecting underrepresented students to STEM opportunities. Find comprehensive, accurate data on STEM programs from federal science agencies.

## Target Agencies & Programs

### National Science Foundation (NSF)
- REU (Research Experience for Undergraduates) — all disciplines, all universities
- REU Sites — search for the REU site finder
- CAREER, GRFP (Graduate Research Fellowship Program)
- NSF INTERN (industry research supplement)
- STEM Education programs (middle/high school focus)

### National Institutes of Health (NIH)
- Summer Internship Program (SIP) — at NIH campus
- Undergraduate Scholarship Program (UGSP)
- NIAID Summer Internship Program
- Community College Summer Enrichment Program (CCSEP)
- Postbac IRTA (Intramural Research Training Award)
- NICHD programs
- NIH Clinical Center programs
- NIDDK summer programs
- Bridges to the Baccalaureate
- Bridges to the Doctorate
- Initiative for Maximizing Student Development (IMSD)

### NOAA (National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration)
- Hollings Scholarship
- Ernest F. Hollings Undergraduate Scholarship
- NOAA Internship Programs
- Sea Grant programs
- NOAA Educational Partnership Program (EPP)

### EPA (Environmental Protection Agency)
- Greater Research Opportunities (GRO) Fellowship
- EPA Internship Program
- STAR Fellowship
- People, Prosperity, and the Planet (P3) program

### USDA (U.S. Department of Agriculture)
- 1890 Scholars Program
- USDA Internships
- Agricultural Research Service student opportunities
- USFS (Forest Service) internships
- NRCS student opportunities

### USGS (U.S. Geological Survey)
- Student career experience programs
- USGS Mendenhall Research Fellowship
- Pathways Internship Program
- Water Resources Research Institute (WRRI) student grants

### Other Federal Science
- FDA internships (food science, biology, chemistry)
- CDC Public Health Associates Program
- CDC/ATSDR internships
- NIST (National Institute of Standards and Technology) internships
- NSA internships (STEM focus)

## Research Instructions

1. Search for each agency's official education/internship page
2. Visit each official program URL — extract real deadlines, stipends, eligibility
3. For NSF REU: focus on the program overview (individual REU sites are too numerous)
4. Note which programs specifically target community college or underrepresented students

## Output

Return ONLY a JSON array following the OUTPUT_SCHEMA. No explanation, no markdown — pure JSON.

Minimum 20 programs. Use verified URLs only.
